Flavors of India

North India

The Royal Kitchens

Characterized by rich, creamy gravies, tandoor-baked breads, and aromatic spices like garam masala. Influenced by Mughal royalty, the cuisine is hearty and indulgent.

Butter Chicken Rogan Josh Naan Dal Makhani
South India

Coastal & Spiced

A vibrant cuisine relying heavily on rice, lentils, coconut, and curry leaves. Known for its complex spice blends, fiery curries, and unique fermented delicacies.

Masala Dosa Idli Sambar Chettinad Chicken Appam
